LEAN Program in WOM​​​​​​

The Lean program strenghtens the culture of continuous improvement and empowers staff to solve problems on a daily basis, creating business improvement opportunities with customers at the forefront of those opportunities.

Some Rules on Reporting Lean Outcomes

  • Any Lean improvement must be in the Lean Repository
  • Lean improvements cannot eliminate headcount
  • If an improvement has not been Money Belt verified by Finance, it cannot be used in the Business Plan
  • The Lean Office provides all program stats (e.g. trained staff, cost savings, cost avoidance, projects completed

WHAT TYPES OF IMPROVEMENTS DO WE DO?

Blank Small Improvements Rapid Improvements Projects
Characteristics
  • The Problem is usually contained within one business unit
  • An implemented solution to a known issue
  • The problem requires a small effort to complete, but doesn't necessarily have a small impact to the business unit
  • The problem is usually contained within one Division
  • The process is straight forward and understood by the working team
  • The problem usually impacts multiple business units & Divisions
  • A large number of hand-offs between staff
  • The process is not straightforward and not completely understood by the working team
  • The root causes are unknown
  • There is a higher variation in the process
Duration Immediate 3-4 Months 6-8 Months
