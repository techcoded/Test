Technical Services

The Technical Services section is primarily responsible for surveys and inspections as well maintenance of standards and permits.

In addition to providing Capital Works with Surveyors and Inspections servies, Technical Services is also responsible for the following:

  • Material Testing
  • Ontario One Call Storm Sewer Locates
  • PUCC Coordination
  • Utility Cut Permits
  • Roadway Inspections
  • Standards
